NGN @ IISER TVM

Blogged on : 19th Dec 2024

First blog! A bunch of us from lab went to the NGN (No Garland Neuroscience) conference, at IISER Trivandrum - from 3rd Dec to 8th Dec, 2024.

Went with Jhilik, Surbhi, Sanika, Prithu, Deb. Jhilik and Deb had talks at the conference, while Surbhi, Sanika and I presented posters. We had soo much fun. My labmates friends are so cool.
